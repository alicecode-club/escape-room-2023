The School for Good and Evil Book Summary

The School for Good and Evil is a fantasy fiction story by Soman Chainani. The story revolves around two girls, Sophie and Agatha, who are polar opposites but become best friends. They are taken to a school where young students are trained to become fairy-tale heroes and villains. The girls are placed in the opposite curriculum from what they expected - Agatha in the School for Good and Sophie in the School for Evil. The story follows their adventures as they navigate through this unconventional school.
